About Own Designs Ltd

We are a UK-based creative studio blending elegance, luxury, and cultural artistry. Our work focuses on fine details, balance, and a premium design experience. Each project is treated with attention to quality, originality, and artistic expression.

Our mission is to deliver designs that are not only visually appealing but also meaningful and memorable. Whether it is traditional henna art, modern digital illustrations, card designs, or interior concepts, we aim to exceed client expectations every time.

  • Bespoke & personalised designs for weddings, events, and businesses
  • Luxury gold & white aesthetics with attention to detail
  • Trusted by 500+ happy clients across UK and international markets
  • Collaborative approach ensuring your vision is perfectly captured
  • High-quality digital and physical design deliverables

Founded in 2020, we have consistently combined creativity with professionalism to bring ideas to life in a refined and elegant manner. Every design tells a story, and we are here to make sure your story is unforgettable.
